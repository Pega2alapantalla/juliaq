About me

Hi there! I’m Julia, a character designer, concept artist and graphic designer based in Spain.

From a young age, I’ve always been drawn to expressive characters and imaginative worlds. I love creating visuals that feel alive—full of personality, emotion, and small details that tell a story beyond the image. Over time, this passion has evolved into a focus on character design and storytelling, exploring the balance between playful, colorful aesthetics and emotional depth.

Education & Background

I studied Fine Arts in Seville and later completed a two-year Master’s in Concept Art and Illustration at the Universal Arts School, which allowed me to refine my skills and expand my artistic approach across different styles and disciplines.

Professional Experience

Alongside my artistic practice, I’ve worked as a graphic designer for two years on the UCAnFly project, collaborating with the European Space Agency. This experience gave me the opportunity to contribute in a multidisciplinary team and apply design in technical and collaborative contexts.

My work is inspired by animation and stories that blend humor with heartfelt moments. I’m especially interested in creating characters and worlds that feel relatable, expressive, and full of life. Currently, I’m working on my professional portfolio and developing personal projects.

When I’m not drawing, I enjoy doing crafts, experimenting with materials, and finding inspiration in everyday life and the people around me.
